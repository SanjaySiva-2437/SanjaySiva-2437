## &nbsp;About

I'm an AI & ML engineering student at SRM IST, Chennai — building things that actually work in the real world. My projects span autonomous drones, computer vision for agriculture, and sports analytics. I'm drawn to problems where software has to interact with messy, unpredictable physical environments.

Currently deep in diffusion models, reinforcement learning, and LLM fine-tuning. When I'm not training models, I'm gaming, listening to music, or debugging something at midnight.
